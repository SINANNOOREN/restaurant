Our Journey

1995

Our Story Beginnings

In 1995, Royal Malabar was born from a passion for authentic Kerala flavors. What started as a small family-run kitchen serving traditional Malabar delicacies quickly became a local favorite. Our commitment to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and age-old recipes made us a household name in Kerala’s culinary scene.

2005

Milestone year

By 2005, Royal Malabar had grown into a well-known restaurant, attracting food lovers from across Kerala. We expanded our menu, introducing signature dishes that fused tradition with creativity. Our focus on exceptional service and unforgettable flavors helped us build a loyal community of diners.

2020

New chapter

By 2020, Royal Malabar had evolved into a fine dining experience, blending tradition with modern luxury. We redefined Malabar cuisine by introducing innovative flavors while preserving its rich heritage. Today, we continue to serve exquisite dishes in an ambiance that reflects Kerala’s royal elegance, offering a dining experience like no other.
